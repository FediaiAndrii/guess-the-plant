    Information about modes

    Easy Mode
    You get 4 answer options. Each wrong answer deducts 25 points.

    Medium Mode
    You get 10 answer options. Each wrong answer deducts 10 points.

    Hard Mode
    You must type the plant's name yourself. Each wrong answer deducts 1 point. If you use a hint, each wrong answer in that round deducts 5 points instead.

    After each round, your score adds to your highscore. Try to reach 1000 points in as few rounds as possible!
